About Me

As a child, I have always tried to understand the world around me by questioning why things are the way they are. I am never content with the idea that everything must and will always be how they are now. During summers between school, I would get depressed about not working on anything new or creating something. This pushed me to become self driven in creating projects for myself. I never accepted things to be one dimensional and felt the need to explore every aspect of them. This curiosity and desire to understand have remained fundamental to the person I am and seek to become.

Interests

Snowboaring I have been snowboarding for about 8 years now. It has been off and on - basically I go whenever I can and always have a blast when I do. Back in college, I used to go on black diamonds. I have to shred again soon.

Playing Guitar I have started playing guitar in the last year. I am able to play a few Beatles songs and know my chords well. I hope to continue learning and progressing with this skill. I hope to be able to make my own music one day.

Running My favorite cardio exercise is to run. There is nothing better than listening to a podcast as you are running up and down hills. The runners high is the best feeling in the world and once that kicks, there is nothing that can bring you down.

Boxing I have gone to some of the best boxing gyms in Northern California in the last year and have loved the amount of body movement and stamina it requires to expend each time. I will get back into it this summer. Muhammad Ali is my favorite all time with Mike Tyson as a close second.

Basketball I have been playing since I was 15 years old and wanted to be a NBA player when I was younger. That was definitely not my fate as I am barely 6 feet tall, but I still love playing with friends and watching my favorite team the Warriors.

Music I pride myself on my 70,000+ spotify minutes and love to create playlists for my friends and family. I have always been drawn to music even though it was not very present in the environment I grew up in. I love seeeing live music in my free time and a couple of years ago, I decided to start learning to play the ukulele and guitar.

Volunteering

I read books, played games like keep the balloon from falling, and guess that celebrity - however, they were celebrities from prior to the 80's so I was a bit lost.

PYB Coach
I have worked with kids by coaching them in basketball, which is a great medium to learn team skills and self esteem. The way I did that was becoming a basketball coach for the local youth league for 3rd and 5th graders. This was extremely fulfilling to me and I will definitely do that again.
